# Contributing to PlanAI Field

© Taner Piri / PiriStrategy. All rights reserved.

PlanAI Field is a proprietary spatial intelligence and field workflow platform developed by PiriStrategy.

## Access policy

This repository is **not open source**. Contributions are accepted only from authorized PiriStrategy collaborators, contractors, or partners operating under a written agreement.

If you do not have an explicit contribution agreement, do **not** submit pull requests, patches, or forks intended for public redistribution.

## Prohibited activities

Without written authorization from PiriStrategy:

- Unauthorized redistribution or publication of source code
- Commercial reuse, white-label deployment, or resale
- Derivative platform cloning or competing product development
- Reverse engineering of protected security, sync, or georeferencing modules
- Unauthorized SaaS or multi-tenant hosting of PlanAI Field

## Protected intellectual property

Contributions may touch protected systems including spatial workflow architecture, GIS/CAD hybrid systems, smart georeferencing logic, spatial synchronization systems, field reporting workflows, AI-assisted planning systems, spatial security architecture, UI/UX concepts, and municipality workflows.

All work product submitted under authorization remains the property of PiriStrategy unless otherwise agreed in writing.

## Security reports

Responsible disclosure for security issues affecting authorized deployments should be directed to PiriStrategy through official channels listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
